Life

Poem about the challenges of life, June 25, 2020

BlogNest Welcomes all of you to my world of blog.

Trillions walk on the sand, of this mighty land. Footprints are left by some, The waves wash them as soon as they come. Meeting people on your way is rare, Making them stay forebver is a dare. Life is a hide and seek, we all have to play, Certainly, only a handful will stay. A picture captures the moment, And we cherish it throughout our lifetime as an ornament. It seems like a nightmare to be apart, From those who shared with me a cherishing part. Words aren't suffice to what i want to say, As I have thousands of words to convey. But, I don't know why I get speechless with my blurry eyes, Acknowledging that this worldis full of disguise. No matter what i will always smile, As life is an island and we have to walk down the aisle, Till the time passes in the clock, And the death says KNOCK-KNOCK.
